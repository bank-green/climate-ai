Find out more about Financial Services Compensation Scheme | This will open in a new tab Financial Services Compensation Scheme Your eligible deposits held by a UK establishment of The Co-operative Bank plc are protected up to a total of *£85,000* by the Financial Services Compensation Scheme, the UK's deposit guarantee scheme. This limit is applied to the total of any deposits you have with the following: The Co-operative Bank, Britannia and smile. Any total deposits you hold above the limit between these brands are unlikely to be covered. Please read further information on the FSCS scheme here or visit their website.
